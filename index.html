KERALA BLOCKCHAIN ACADEMY INNOVATION CLUB CELL

KERALA BLOCKCHAIN ACADEMY INNOVATION CLUB(KBAIC) is a novel initiative by kerala blockchain academy under the Indian Istitute of Technology and Management-kerala(IITM-K). This initiative has been put forward to help students from academic instititions across the country to explore deep into the potential of Blockchain Technology.
KBAIC provides an oppurtunity to work closely with experts and experiment the latest advancements in this domain. MES AIMAT is a member of KBA Innovation Club and the first institution to start a Blockchain cell.

OBJECTIVES

The aim of the club is to create awareness about Blockchain and to enhance students skills in blockchain technology. This will also helps students to build their career in the disruptive world of blockchain
